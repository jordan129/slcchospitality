### Sushi Offer – Every Tuesday
- Enjoy 30% off all sushi, including sushi/sashimi sets and special maki.
- Advanced booking is required to avail of the offer.
- Offer is valid for dine-in only and applies to food orders only.
- Drinks, desserts, and extras are excluded from the discount.
 
### 2-4-1 Happy Hour – Every Thursday to Saturday
- Buy 1 Get 1 Free on selected drinks from 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM.
- Applicable on glasses of wines, cocktails, and mocktails.
- The free drink must be of equal or lesser value than the purchased one.
- Offer valid for dine-in only.
- Walk-ins are welcome; no booking required.
 
### Noodles, Rice & Donburi Offer – Every Wednesday & Sunday
- Enjoy 20% off all noodles, rice, and donburi dishes.
- Advanced booking is required to receive the discount.
- Offer valid for dine-in only.
- Drinks, desserts, and side orders are not included.
 
### Additional Conditions
- Offers cannot be used in conjunction with any other promotions, vouchers, or discounts.
- Management reserves the right to refuse service to any customer found violating these terms.
- In case of disputes, the management’s decision is final.
- Any fraudulent or abusive use of these offers may result in cancellation without prior notice.
- Offers are valid for a limited time and may be changed or withdrawn at any time.
